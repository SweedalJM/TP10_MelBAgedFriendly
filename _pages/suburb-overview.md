Melbourne has people from 104 cultures.</p> City of Melbourne is a home to all age groups.<br /><p>It is a place for children, young adults, families and older adults.</p> <img style="width: 100%; max-width:1200px; display: block;opacity:0;margin:0px;" src="data:image/svg+xml;base64,PHN2ZyB4bWxucz0iaHR0cDovL3d3dy53My5vcmcvMjAwMC9zdmciIHdpZHRoPSIxMjAwIiBoZWlnaHQ9IjgwMCIgPjwvc3ZnPg==" alt="Slider" />
